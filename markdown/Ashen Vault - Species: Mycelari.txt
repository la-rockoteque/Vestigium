# **Mycelari Traits**
Your Mycelari character has the following racial traits.

## **Ability Score Increase**
Your **Constitution** increases by **2**, and your **Wisdom or Intelligence** increases by **1**.

## **Age**
Mycelari do not age **in the same way as most humanoids**. Their **bodies continuously renew through fungal growth**, making their lifespans **erratic**, but most live between **100 to 300 years**, though some **dissolve and regrow as entirely new beings over time**.

## **Alignment**
Most Mycelari are **neutral**, believing that **existence is an unbroken cycle**. Some lean **lawful**, tending to **the delicate balance of symbiosis**, while others are **chaotic**, embracing the **uncontrolled sprawl of fungal life**.

## **Size**
Mycelari are **Medium humanoids**, standing between **4‚Äô8‚Äù and 6‚Äô2‚Äù**, their **bodies shaped as much by their fungal lineage as by natural form**.

## **Speed**
Your base walking speed is **30 feet**.

- You have **resistance to poison damage** and **advantage on saving throws against disease and airborne toxins**.  
- You do not require **traditional food**‚Äîinstead, you **absorb nutrients from decomposing organic matter** over time.

- You can **reattach severed limbs** by allowing fungal tendrils to **stitch them back over time (1 hour per limb)**.
