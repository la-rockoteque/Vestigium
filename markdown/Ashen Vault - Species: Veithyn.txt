# **The Veythin – Dwellers of the Shadowed Veil**
*"We are the dusk between day and night, the echo before silence."*

The **Veythin** are a people bound to the **borders of the Nevermourn**, where the fabric of reality **thins and darkens**. Though humanoid in form, they **carry the essence of shadow itself**, manifesting as **subtle, eerie traits** that mark them as different from other mortals.  

Their **flowing, shadow-like hair**, **deep black-veined skin**, and **long, refined faces** give them an otherworldly elegance. **Born in the twilight between existence and oblivion, the Veythin move with unnatural grace, their very presence evoking the feeling of an approaching storm.**  

---

## **Physical Characteristics**
- **Height & Build:** **5'8" to 6'6"**, possessing **long, athletic** frames.
- **Face & Eyes:** **Elongated features** with **shadow-laced, abyssal black eyes** that **shift in the dim light**.
- **Hair:** Appears as **flowing shadow**, **drifting and curling like smoke** even in still air.
- **Skin & Veins:** Their **pale gray to obsidian-black skin** is laced with **visible black veins**, pulsing faintly with an **eerie glow** when near **the Nevermourn**.
- **Presence:** **Subtly unsettling**, as though **half-there, half-forgotten**—their forms sometimes flicker at the edges of perception.

---

# **The Borderlands of Nevermourn**
*"To stand at the edge of shadow is to be part of both worlds and neither."*

The **Veythin** reside on the **outermost borders of the Nevermourn**, a land where **reality and shadow intertwine**. Their villages and cities are built along the **"Veil Rift"**, where light itself bends, and the air carries a **whispered resonance of things long lost**.  

### **Life on the Borderlands**
- **Veythin settlements are constructed of dark stone**, carved from the **Veil Rift**, infused with **latent shadow essence**.
- They possess a **natural attunement to darkness**, enabling them to **move unseen** or **vanish into shadowed spaces** with ease.
- Though **not innately malevolent**, Veythin are viewed with **superstition and fear** by outsiders due to their proximity to **the Nevermourn**.

### **The Shadowed Pact**
Legends tell that **long ago, the Veythin struck a silent accord with the Nevermourn itself**—an understanding that allows them to **exist at the border without succumbing to the consuming dark**. Whether this pact is **an ancient magic, a divine blessing, or a forgotten curse**, none can say.

---

# **Veythin Traits**
As a Veythin, you gain the following racial traits.

## **Ability Score Increase**
Your **Dexterity** increases by **2**, and your **Wisdom** increases by **1**.

## **Age**
Veythin mature at the same rate as humans but can live up to **200 years**, their lifespans stretched by their connection to the **Nevermourn**.

## **Alignment**
Most Veythin lean toward **neutral alignments**, following a **philosophy of balance** between shadow and light. Some become **mystics**, others **warriors of the Veil**, while a few **succumb to the call of the dark**.

## **Size**
Veythin are **Medium-sized humanoids**, ranging from **5'8" to 6'6"**.

## **Speed**
Your base walking speed is **35 feet**.

---

## **Innate Traits**
### **🌑 Shadowborn**
The Nevermourn’s influence lingers within you, making you more than just mortal.  
- You have **advantage on Stealth checks** while in **dim light or darkness**.  
- You can attempt to **hide even when lightly obscured** by **shadows, mist, or ambient darkness**.

### **👁️ Veilvision**
Your eyes **pierce the dark**, perceiving **what others cannot**.  
- You have **darkvision out to 120 feet**.  
- Magical darkness does **not** impede your vision.  

### **🔹 Flickering Form**
Your presence is **not entirely fixed in reality**, allowing you to slip between moments.  
- As a **bonus action**, you can **partially phase out of existence** until the start of your next turn.
  - While in this state, **attack rolls against you have disadvantage**.
  - You can **move through enemy spaces**, treating them as difficult terrain.
  - Once you use this ability, you must **finish a short or long rest** before using it again.

### **🖤 Echoed Steps**
The **Nevermourn’s whispers** follow you wherever you go.  
- Once per **long rest**, when you fail a Stealth check, you can **reroll the check**.
- Your **footsteps make no sound**, even when running or stepping on fragile surfaces.

# **Veythin Subraces**
Veythin are shaped by **how deeply they embrace the Nevermourn**, with distinct **cultural paths**.

### **1. Duskwardens (Guardians of the Veil)**
*"We watch, we listen, we hold the line."*
- Tasked with guarding **the borders of Nevermourn**, ensuring **no horrors cross over**.
- Have **heightened danger sense**, granting **advantage on Perception checks** related to **detecting movement in darkness**.
- Often serve as **scouts, watchers, or Veil-walkers**.

### **2. Shroudcallers (Keepers of Shadow Magic)**
*"The darkness does not speak to us; we *are* the darkness."*
- Have an **innate connection to shadow magic**, capable of **manipulating darkness like a tangible force**.
- Gain **minor innate spellcasting**, allowing them to cast **Thaumaturgy** and **Darkness (once per long rest)**.
- Serve as **scholars, mystics, or conduits of forgotten shadow lore**.

### **3. Umbradancers (Echoes Between Worlds)**
*"To move like the wind, to strike like the night."*
- Specialize in **speed and unpredictability**, making them **masters of hit-and-run combat**.
- Gain **+10 feet to movement speed** and the ability to **Dash as a bonus action**.
- Often found as **assassins, infiltrators, or ghostly wanderers**.
